## Core Financial Planning Knowledge

### Australia Superannuation Framework

**Key Numbers (2025-26)**
| Parameter | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Concessional cap | $30,000/year (indexed) |
| Non-concessional cap | $120,000/year |
| Bring-forward (under 75) | $360,000 over 3 years |
| Super guarantee rate | 11.5% (rises to 12% Jul 2025) |
| Low-income super tax offset | $500 (for those earning <$37K) |
| Division 293 threshold | $250,000 income (extra 15% tax on concessional contributions) |
| Super balance limit (NCC) | $1.9M total super balance caps NCCs |
| Transfer balance cap | $1.9M (into pension phase) |

**Tax Rates in Super**
- Contributions (concessional): 15% tax inside fund
- Investment earnings: 15% inside accumulation phase
- Pension phase: 0% tax on investment earnings and pension payments
- Death benefits: depend on dependant status of beneficiary

**Optimal Contribution Strategy for Anthony**
1. Salary sacrifice (or personal concessional) up to $30K cap every year
2. Check carry-forward unused cap from prior years (available if super balance <$500K)
3. Non-concessional contributions when empire generates significant surplus: $120K in one hit or $360K bring-forward
4. Spouse contributions if applicable: $540 tax offset for contributing spouse (spouse income <$40K)

**When to Consider SMSF**
- SMSF makes financial sense at super balance >$250,000 (economies of scale)
- Advantages: direct share ownership, property inside super (commercial only unless LRBA), crypto exposure options, control over investment strategy
- Disadvantages: trustee responsibilities, annual audit cost (~$2,000-$3,000), time burden
- Ideal trigger: when business premises or commercial property is considered for purchase inside super

### Insurance Framework for Business Owners

**Priority Order for Anthony**
1. **Income Protection** — most critical. Empire is entirely dependent on Anthony's capacity to make decisions.
   - Cover: 75% of income, up to 2-year benefit period minimum (5-year preferred)
   - Waiting period: 30 days (inside super) or 14 days (outside super)
   - Own occupation definition (not "any occupation")
   - Cost estimate: $100-$300/month inside super, $150-$400/month outside
   - Inside vs outside super: inside uses pre-tax contributions but benefit taxed; outside uses post-tax but benefit tax-free

2. **Total and Permanent Disability (TPD)** — any occupation definition minimum
   - Covers inability to work in any profession
   - Cost: relatively low for age <45

3. **Life Insurance** — necessary only if dependants or debt
   - If Anthony has dependants or large mortgage: $1M minimum
   - If no dependants and no mortgage: minimal cover acceptable

4. **Trauma/Critical Illness** — optional but valuable
   - Pays lump sum on diagnosis of cancer, heart attack, stroke
   - Allows recovery time without income stress

**Insurance Inside vs Outside Super**
- Inside super: premiums paid from pre-tax contributions (efficient), but death benefit rules more complex
- Outside super: more flexible, TPD own-occupation only available outside super (post-2014)
- Recommendation: income protection outside super (own-occupation, tax-free benefit)

### Estate Planning Framework

**Minimum Estate Planning for Business Owners**

1. **Will**
   - Current: does Anthony have a will? Critical action if not.
   - Should address: business assets, digital assets, domain names, empire equity
   - Update: every 3 years or after major life change

2. **Enduring Power of Attorney**
   - Financial: who controls empire decisions if Anthony is incapacitated?
   - Medical: who makes health decisions?
   - Must be registered in WA (State Records Office)

3. **Superannuation Death Benefit Nomination**
   - Binding nomination: goes to specified person regardless of trustee discretion
   - Non-binding: trustee decides — can result in super going to estate (taxed)
   - Recommendation: binding nomination to dependant(s) or estate
   - Review every 3 years (binding nominations lapse)

4. **Digital Asset Estate Plan**
   - Who gets access to the empire, servers, API keys, social accounts?
   - Password manager with trusted person having access method
   - Not the passwords themselves in the will — a sealed letter or vault reference

### FIRE Planning — Australian Context

**FIRE Numbers**
- Annual expenses: $80,000 (comfortable Australian lifestyle)
- FIRE number (25x rule): $2,000,000
- Safe withdrawal rate: 4% ($80,000/year from $2M)
- Super equivalent: $1.9M in pension phase = $76,000/year tax-free

**Timeline to FIRE**
- Conservative: 7 years at $50K/month empire revenue
- Aggressive: 4 years at $100K+/month
- With property and business valuations: potentially 3-4 years

**Australian FIRE Optimisations**
- Super: use as core retirement vehicle (0% tax in pension phase)
- Investment bonds: after 10 years, withdrawals tax-free (alternative to super)
- Franking credits: Australian dividend stocks yield 6-8% grossed up
- Spousal splitting: maximise both partners' tax-free thresholds in retirement
