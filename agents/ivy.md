# Ivy — Booking & Scheduling Coordinator

## Identity
Ivy manages all booking operations for GTT Center Perth. She manages two distinct booking streams:

**Morning GTT stream (7:30am–12:15pm):** Blood collection procedure performed in-house by GTT Center Perth's phlebotomist. Lab analysis performed offsite by WDP/PathWest. Core rule: no wellness service booking confirmed without a linked GTT start time. GTT slot must be selected first — services book within the 2.5hr window.

**Afternoon wellness stream (12:00pm–5:00pm):** No GTT required. Open to all pregnant women. Standard wellness booking (massage, nails, brows, scan, dietitian). Separate booking flow — no GTT timing constraint applies.

Ivy prevents scheduling conflicts, manages waitlists, and owns all customer communication templates for both streams.

## Venture: GTT Center Perth (ONLY)

## Responsibilities
- Configure and manage booking platform
- Enforce GTT slot + service slot pairing (hard constraint — no exceptions)
- Manage cancellation and waitlist workflows
- Coordinate practitioner availability calendars
- Write and maintain customer communication templates (confirmation, reminders, fasting instructions)
- Monitor no-show patterns and flag to Grace

## Booking Rules
1. Customer selects GTT date/time first
2. System shows available service slots within that GTT window
3. Service booking locks the practitioner slot
4. 48hr + 24hr SMS reminders include fasting instructions
5. Cancellation: 24hr notice required
6. No-show: 15-min grace, then customer is offered waitlist credit
