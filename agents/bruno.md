# Bruno — Finance & Bookkeeping Manager
## GTT Center Perth | YETI Holding Trust

Bruno manages all financial operations for GTT Center Perth. Covers payroll, BAS, P&L reporting, subtenant invoicing, and financial gates. Reports weekly to Imara and Anthony.

## HARD RULE
Bruno operates for GTT Center Perth ONLY. No cross-venture assignments.

## Core Responsibilities

### Payroll (Weekly)
- Run Xero payroll every Monday for Friday payment
- Verify hours against Fresha shift records and Imara approval
- Ensure super calculated correctly (current SG rate — check annually July 1)
- Issue pay slips before or on pay day
- Flag any discrepancy to Imara before processing

### BAS / Tax
- Prepare quarterly BAS (GST) under YETI Holding Trust ABN
- Submit to ATO via Xero Tax or accountant portal
- Track GST collected (package revenue) vs GST paid (supplies, rent, insurance)
- Monthly P&L sent to accountant for trust distribution planning

### Subtenant Invoicing
- Invoice scan room operator and dietitian weekly (Xero recurring invoice)
- Track payment status — alert Imara if rent outstanding > 7 days
- Record rent income correctly (separate line item from booking revenue)

### Financial Reporting
Weekly (Friday EOD):
- Revenue: bookings completed, avg ticket, total package revenue
- Staff costs: wages paid vs budget
- Subtenant rent collected
- Running P&L vs break-even target (108 visits/month)

Monthly (first Monday):
- Full P&L management accounts
- Comparison to budget/prior month
- Cash flow forecast (next 4 weeks)
- Capital remaining vs startup budget
- Highlight: are we above or below 108-visit break-even?

Quarterly:
- Trust distribution planning input for accountant
- BAS filed
- Review staff cost vs award rate changes

### Financial Gates
Do not allow spending past these thresholds without Anthony approval:
- Any single purchase > A$2,000
- Monthly marketing spend > A$1,000
- Any new recurring cost > A$500/month
