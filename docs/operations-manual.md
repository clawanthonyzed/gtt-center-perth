## 1. DAILY OPERATIONS CADENCE

### 7:00am — Imara Opens
- [ ] Disarm alarm, unlock venue
- [ ] Check Fresha dashboard: today's bookings, any cancellations overnight, waitlist notifications
- [ ] Review any emails/calls from previous evening
- [ ] Switch on: lights, HVAC, lounge music (low, calming playlist), diffuser (pregnancy-safe — lavender/citrus only, NOT eucalyptus or clary sage)
- [ ] Brief morning weather check — note if parking conditions unusual (advise patients)
- [ ] Send morning summary to self: today's patient count, service mix, any special notes

### 7:15am — Receptionist Arrives
- [ ] Receives venue handover from Imara (verbal + Fresha check)
- [ ] Prepare lounge: herbal teas out, water available, snack station stocked (check stock levels)
- [ ] Prepare welcome pack for each patient: referral form, GTT information sheet, QR code card (Google review)
- [ ] Confirm today's 75g glucose solutions are out of fridge (need 20 min room temp before use) — coordinate with phlebotomist
- [ ] Check Fresha: confirm all bookings have mobile number and fasting reminder was sent
- [ ] For any patient without confirmed fasting reminder received: call immediately

### 7:15am — Phlebotomist Arrives
- [ ] Collection room daily checklist (see gtt-clinical-protocol.md §4)
- [ ] Confirm day's GTT slot times match Fresha calendar
- [ ] Brief coordination with receptionist: today's patient names and slot times
- [ ] Prepare labelling supplies for all patients
- [ ] Confirm fridge temperature and log

### 7:30am — Service Staff Arrive (Massage, Nails, Brows)
- [ ] Set up treatment rooms: linens fresh, equipment sanitised from previous day
- [ ] Check Fresha for their individual appointment schedule
- [ ] Confirm any patient-specific notes (e.g., neck pain, late trimester, preferred pressure)
- [ ] Restock any professional products needed

### 8:00am — First Patients Arrive (Slot A, if applicable)

**Reception Arrival Flow (per patient):**
1. Greet by name: "Good morning [Name], welcome to GTT Center Perth!"
2. Confirm fasting: "When did you last eat or drink anything except water?"
   - If fasting < 8 hours: DO NOT proceed to T=0 draw. Consult Imara. Offer to reschedule.
   - If fasting confirmed: proceed
3. Collect referral form and Medicare card — scan and return originals
4. Offer water immediately ("The phlebotomist recommends drinking water right now — it makes the draw easier")
5. Escort to collection room, introduce to phlebotomist
6. After T=0 draw: escort to lounge, offer tea menu, brief on their morning ("You have your massage at 8:30, then nails at 9:15, your second blood draw is at 9:00 — I'll come and get you")
7. Manage all escort transitions throughout the morning

### 9:00am–11:00am — Peak Operations
- All 4 slots potentially active simultaneously
- Receptionist tracks all T=60 and T=120 draw times on a physical whiteboard (as backup to Fresha)
- Draw alert system: set phone reminders for each patient's T=60 and T=120 (5 minutes before)
- If any service runs over time: communicate immediately to phlebotomist, never miss a draw window
  - Draw window tolerance: ±5 minutes of T=60, ±10 minutes of T=120

### 11:00am–12:00pm — Wind-Down
- Last patients completing T=120 draws
- Phlebotomist prepares final specimen batch for courier dispatch
- Service staff completing final treatments
- Receptionist processes final payments

### 12:00pm–12:30pm — Close Operations
- All patients have departed
- Phlebotomist: dispatch final specimens, clean and pack collection room, complete logs
- Service staff: strip and restock treatment rooms, laundry into hamper, restock supplies
- Receptionist: finalise Fresha end-of-day report, close payments, print tomorrow's booking sheet

### 12:30pm — EOD Wrap (Imara)
- [ ] Fresha daily report: bookings completed, revenue, no-shows, cancellations
- [ ] Review tomorrow's bookings: any gaps, any patients needing reminder call
- [ ] Staff sign-off: confirm everyone's hours are correct in Fresha/Xero
- [ ] Check snack/product stock — add to order list if low
- [ ] Lock collection room (phlebotomist has locked, Imara double-checks)
- [ ] Lock venue, set alarm

## 3. BOOKING MANAGEMENT PROTOCOL

### Booking Channels
- **Primary:** GTT Center Perth website → Fresha booking widget
- **Secondary:** Phone (receptionist enters into Fresha directly)
- **Tertiary:** Referral card walk-in (rare — direct to phone)

### GTT Slot Pairing Rule
Every booking MUST include a GTT start time selection before services can be added.  
Services display only for the window available after the selected GTT start time.

**Fresha workaround (until custom booking page built):**
1. GTT slot is the first mandatory service selected (created as a 0-cost "consultation" in Fresha)
2. All other services are bookable only as add-ons to the GTT consultation
3. Receptionist reviews every new online booking same day to confirm pairing is correct
4. If pairing is missing: receptionist calls patient within 4 hours to confirm GTT time

### Deposit Policy
- A$30 non-refundable deposit collected at booking
- Fresha Pay (card) — EFTPOS available at venue for balance
- Deposit refunded/rescheduled if cancellation ≥24 hours prior

### Cancellation Workflow
1. Cancellation comes in via Fresha/phone
2. Receptionist checks: ≥24 hours → refund deposit, free rescheduled slot
3. < 24 hours → deposit forfeited, note in Fresha
4. No-show → full session charged (Fresha card-on-file charge)
5. Waitlist: if cancellation occurs, immediately notify first person on waitlist (Fresha automated + personal call)

### Overbooking Rule
Maximum 4 GTT patients per day with 1 phlebotomist.  
When 2nd phlebotomist hired: maximum 8 per day.  
Never accept a 5th booking without confirming phlebotomist cover.

## 5. QUALITY CONTROL

### Customer Experience Standards
- Every patient greeted by name within 30 seconds of arrival
- Lounge snack station restocked daily (never empty)
- Treatment rooms inspected before every appointment (fresh linen, clean, temperature appropriate)
- Post-visit: every patient receives a Google review request (verbal from receptionist + QR card)
- Target: 20+ Google reviews per month in first 3 months

### Service Quality
- Massage: Imara conducts unannounced client satisfaction check-in for first 10 bookings
- Nails: No acetone products. 9-free or 12-free polishes only. List approved products on order sheet.
- Brows: Patch test offered for tinting (PPD-free products mandatory)
- All services: pregnancy-specific intake form completed on first visit, updated each subsequent visit

### Clinical Quality
- All GTT collections reviewed by phlebotomist daily: draw times within protocol, labelling correct
- WDP/PathWest specimen rejection rate tracked monthly (target: <1%)
- Any specimen rejection → immediate root cause investigation

### Financial Quality
- Fresha daily report matches actual payments processed (Imara spot-check weekly)
- No walkouts without payment — all payments processed at end of visit
- Subtenant rent: if not received by Wednesday of each week, reminder sent Thursday, escalate to Imara Friday

## 6. INCIDENT MANAGEMENT

### Clinical Incident (patient unwell during GTT)
1. Phlebotomist assesses immediately
2. Receptionist calls Imara
3. If emergency: call 000, Imara stays with patient
4. Post-incident: complete incident register within 24 hours
5. Notify WDP/PathWest clinical team same day
6. If patient required ambulance: notify accountant (insurance claim), notify solicitor

### Non-Clinical Incident (slip/fall, property damage, complaint)
1. Imara first response
2. Document in incident register
3. Photos taken if relevant (property damage)
4. Customer complaint: Imara calls same day, resolves within 48 hours
5. If insurance claim needed: notify Gallagher/BizCover broker

### Data Breach
1. Imara assesses: is patient data involved?
2. If yes: notify solicitor within 24 hours
3. Under Privacy Act: notify OAIC if breach is serious and likely to cause harm
4. Do not notify patients before getting legal advice

## GTT SCHEDULING TIMETABLES

### Key Rules
- Customers arrive 10-15 min before their appointment time
- Customer finishes glucose drink in the LOUNGE (not collection room) -- frees collection room for next customer
- Services do NOT get interrupted for the 1-hour blood draw -- EXCEPT hairdressing
- Phlebotomist times the draw around service completion, within T+60-90 window
- Services must finish 5 min before scheduled draw
- 90 usable minutes of services per GTT client (2 x 45-min windows)
