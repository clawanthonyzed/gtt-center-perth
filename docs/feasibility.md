## YETI Holding Trust | GTT Center Perth
### Version 1.0 | June 2026

## Executive Summary

GTT Center Perth proposes to operate WA's first purpose-built Gestational Diabetes Test (GTT/OGTT) wellness centre — combining mandatory pathology collection with premium maternity wellness services (massage, nails, brows, beauty) to transform a 2.5-hour clinical wait into a premium self-care experience for pregnant women.

**Feasibility Verdict: VIABLE** — with moderate risk, primarily execution-dependent.

| Dimension | Rating | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| Market demand | ✅ Strong | ~215 GTT tests/week in Perth. Zero dedicated GTT wellness venues in WA. |
| Financial viability | ✅ Positive | Operational break-even at 108 visits/month. Month 1 target exceeds this. |
| Clinical compliance | ✅ Achievable | WDP NATA umbrella model well-established. Cert III phlebotomist available. |
| Competition | ✅ Low (WA) | No direct WA competitor. Nearest concept: MIWM (NSW/VIC). |
| Startup capital | ⚠️ Moderate | A$209K–431K required. Fundable from trust + investor if needed. |
| Execution risk | ⚠️ Moderate | Depends on WDP accreditation timeline, staff quality, and waitlist conversion. |
| Regulatory | ✅ Manageable | Known framework: NATA, ACSQHC, Fair Work, WA health regs. No novel approvals. |

### 1.2 Target Demographic

**Primary:** Pregnant women aged 25–42, weeks 24–28, residing in inner-western Perth suburbs.

**Psychographic profile:**
- Higher disposable income (Subiaco, Nedlands, Claremont, Cottesloe, Floreat median income A$110K–170K household)
- Values premium experience and self-care
- Dreads the GTT test due to the 2.5-hour wait and clinical environment
- Seeks natural/holistic maternity care alongside conventional medicine
- Active on Instagram; takes recommendations from midwives and OB/GYNs
- Already spending on maternity massage, prenatal yoga, pregnancy photography

**Secondary:** High-risk women screened early (first trimester, Weeks 12–16), often requiring repeat GTT.

## 6. Strategic Feasibility

### 6.1 Competitive Moat (Why GTT Center Perth Wins Long-Term)

1. **Clinical trust barrier:** NATA-accredited phlebotomist is not easily replicated. Day spas cannot do this.
2. **Midwife referral lock-in:** Reed builds exclusive referral relationships. Once a midwife practice trusts GTT Center Perth, they will not simultaneously refer to a competitor.
3. **WDP relationship:** First in WA to establish the model. WDP will prefer not to run two competing LCC agreements in the same suburb.
4. **Brand + experience:** Premium lounge feel, known by name among Perth's antenatal community. Word-of-mouth compounds.
5. **Data asset:** Fresha customer base grows over time. 300 patients in Year 1 × NPS 70+ = powerful referral engine.
