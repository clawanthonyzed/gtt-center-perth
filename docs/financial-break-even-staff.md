## Receptionist / Manager Role

This is a **multi-role position**. One hire covers:
- Reception and check-in
- Sales (upselling services and add-ons at booking and arrival)
- Barista (coffee, herbal teas for partners/cafe)
- Food service (snack station, cafe items)
- Clinic assistant (GTT coordination, escorting patients, draw reminders)
- Day-to-day manager (opens venue, manages staff schedule, Fresha oversight)

**Award level:** Clerks Award MA000002 Level 2–3. If the role includes staff supervision, classify at Level 3 ($27.87/hr base = $34.84/hr casual = A$58,500/yr at 30hr/wk including super).

**Hire sequence:** This is the FIRST physical hire. Everything else waits until this role is filled and trained.

## Package Pricing Model

**Philosophy:** Set packages. GTT Center Perth controls price. Client gets flexibility within the package. Venue fee is embedded — inclusions appear free.

| Package | All-In Price | What Client Sees |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| GTT Essential (lounge only) | A$25 | "Lounge access, herbal tea, birth plan tablet — included" |
| GTT Comfort (1 service) | A$25 + service | Client picks any service |
| GTT Day (2 services) | A$25 + 2 services | Client picks two |
| GTT Luxe (massage + nails) | A$290 | "Pregnancy massage + gel manicure — everything included" |

**Post-GTT discount:** 10% off any additional service or retail item booked AFTER glucose drink administered (T+15). Triggers upsell conversation at the moment clients are seated and relaxed.

**Morning priority:** GTT client slots locked until 10:00am. General public can book afternoon slots and any unfilled morning gaps released at 10:00am on the day.

## Staff Downtime Protocol

Staff with no booked client:
1. Clean and prep treatment room for next booking
2. Restock professional products and consumables
3. Review next client notes and special requirements
4. Cross-train on adjacent service (e.g., beauty therapist learns brow waxing)
5. Create social media content (treatment room setup, product features)
6. Complete product knowledge or mandatory training modules
7. Assist receptionist — client coordination, cafe prep, stock orders
8. Receptionist assigns from this list — no unstructured downtime
