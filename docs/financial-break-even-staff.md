# GTT Center Perth — Financial Break-Even: Staff Employment Model

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-06-09
**Model:** Casual/Part-time employees (NOT room rental subtenants)

> This model assumes GTT Center Perth directly employs all practitioners. All service revenue flows to GTT Center Perth. Venue has full control over quality, scheduling, and branding. Payroll is the primary cost driver.

---

## Staffing Model Decision

**Chosen model: Staff employees (casual or part-time)**
- Venue employs all service staff directly
- Venue sets pricing, hours, uniforms, and standards
- Staff receive Award wages + 12% super
- Casual loading = 25% on top of base rate (in lieu of annual/sick leave)
- Flexibility: scale hours week to week based on bookings
- Risk: higher fixed cost than room rental; requires more bookings to break even

**Alternative (NOT chosen): Room rental**
- Practitioners run own businesses, pay weekly room rent to venue
- Lower fixed cost; less operational control

## Per-Employee Break-Even: Services Needed

### Receptionist / Manager (A$56,237/year)

The receptionist does not generate direct service revenue — they support all services. Break-even is measured by total venue revenue the role enables.

- Receptionist annual cost: A$56,237
- Services/year the role must enable: 56,237 / $120 average = **469 services/year = 9 services/week**
- As soon as the venue does 9+ paid services per week, the receptionist pays for themselves
- With 5 GTT patients/day x 5 days x average 1.5 services booked = 37.5 services/week — receptionist covered 4x over from week 1

**Dual-role option:** A receptionist who can also perform beauty services generates direct revenue AND supports operations. Strong recommendation for launch phase.

### Massage Therapist x 1 (A$62,774/year)

| Service | Price | Services/week needed | Sessions/day (5-day) |
|---------|-------|---------------------|----------------------|
| 45-min massage | A$120 | 524/yr = **10.1/wk** | 2.0/day |
| 60-min massage | A$145 | 433/yr = **8.3/wk** | 1.7/day |

Each therapist needs only **8-10 booked massages per week (1.7-2.0 per day)** to break even.
A therapist can do 6 x 45min massages in a 5-hour shift.

**Revenue potential per therapist (full 5hr shift):**
- 6 x 45min massages x $120 = $720/day = $3,600/week = $187,200/year
- Gross margin above wages: $187,200 - $62,774 = **A$124,426/year per therapist**
- With 2 therapists: gross revenue $374,400; gross margin $249,000

### Phlebotomist x 1 (A$57,200/year, 25hrs/week — Option B only)

Under Option A (PathWest/WDP satellite), phlebotomist cost = A$0 (provided by APA).
Under Option B (own ACC), phlebotomist is a strategic cost, not a direct revenue driver.

**Option B phlebotomist break-even logic:**
- Phlebotomist enables GTT Center Perth to run the schedule on its own terms
- Revenue from ACC: ~A$10/patient x 5/day x 250 days = A$12,500/year (does not cover wages)
- The value is operational control + premium package conversion
- Break-even: A$57,200 / A$150 premium package uplift = 381 extra premium upgrades/year = 1.5/day
- **Phlebotomist pays for themselves when just 2 patients/day choose a premium package over standard**

## Revenue Break-Even Analysis

### Assumptions
- All service revenue flows to GTT Center Perth (staff model — 100%)
- Average service price: A$120 (blended)
- Operating days: 250/year (5 days/week x 50 weeks)
- GTT venue fee: A$30/patient (separate from pathology Medicare billing)
- 5 GTT patients/day

### Payroll-Only Break-Even
- Total annual payroll: A$589,453
- Services/year at A$120 avg: 589,453 / 120 = **4,912 services/year**
- Per operating day: 4,912 / 250 = **19.6 services/day**

### Full Break-Even (Payroll + Overhead)
Overhead estimate (rent ~A$60K, utilities A$10K, consumables A$15K, insurance A$5K, marketing A$10K):
- Total overhead (excl. payroll): ~A$100,000/year
- **Total break-even revenue: A$689,453/year**
- Services/year at A$120 avg: **5,745 services/year**
- **Per day: ~23 services/day**
