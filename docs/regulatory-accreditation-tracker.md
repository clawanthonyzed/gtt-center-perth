## Summary — What's Actually Blocking

Per the current model (Option A, not building an in-house accredited lab), the regulatory/accreditation burden is genuinely lighter than a full pathology-lab build would require. The real blocking items, in dependency order:

1. **Pathology partnership confirmation** (WDP reply awaited) — unlocks phlebotomist credentialing, collection room compliance sign-off, and the Medicare billing arrangement confirmation
2. **Physical venue location** — unlocks council permits, food business notification, WorkCover registration timing, and all fit-out-dependent compliance items
3. **Solicitor engagement** — unlocks employment contracts, reviewed privacy policy/consent form, and lease review

None of these are unusual or high-risk for this type of business under the current Option A model — the tracker above exists to make sure nothing is silently assumed complete.
