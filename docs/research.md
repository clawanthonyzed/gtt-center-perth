## Regulatory Summary

### Keepsake 3D Ultrasound
- NOT under AHPRA / NRAS
- Legal as entertainment/photography service in all Australian states
- No diagnostic claims permitted
- ASA has lobbied for regulation but none enacted as of 2026
- Perth already has 4+ operating studios with no regulatory issues reported

### Pathology Collection
- Phlebotomists: no statutory registration required. Cert III in Pathology Collection (HLT37215) is industry standard
- To operate own Approved Collection Centre: NATA accreditation required + Medicare approval
- **Partner model bypasses all accreditation requirements**
- PathWest and WDP both operate satellite/mobile collection points

### Beauty Services
- Massage: no WA state licence, but therapist insurance required
- Nails: no WA licence required (Cert III Nail Technology preferred)
- Hair: Certificate III Hairdressing required for employed hairdressers; subtenants self-responsible
- Food handling: basic council registration if food/drinks served
