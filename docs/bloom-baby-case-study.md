## Launch Strategy: Free Scanning + Staff Training

**The challenge:** A scan operator needs 50–200 supervised scans before working independently.

**GTT Center Perth launch solution:**
1. Advertise for a staff member with existing 3D scan experience (preferred) OR a willing learner
2. Offer **free 3D scans on opening week/month** to build supervised scan count
3. Market the free scans as a grand opening promotion — generates buzz, fills opening schedule, trains the operator
4. Once 50+ supervised scans completed, transition to paid service
5. Consider inviting experienced Perth scan operator to do first 20–30 sessions to validate technique

**Advertising copy for hire:** "Seeking 3D keepsake ultrasound operator for premium maternity wellness centre. Experience preferred — training provided for the right candidate. Morning shifts, premium environment, no diagnostics."

## Equipment Selection

**Option 1 — Refurbished from TGA-registered Australian distributor (RECOMMENDED FIRST):**
| Model | Source | Price (AUD) | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------------|-------|
| SonoScape S20 (refurb) | Sonologic AU (sonologic.com.au) | A$8,000–18,000 | Strong 3D/4D, AU support |
| Mindray DC-80 (refurb) | Mindray AU distributor | A$12,000–25,000 | Excellent brand recognition |
| Samsung WS80A (refurb) | AU refurb dealers | A$18,000–30,000 | Gold standard image quality |

**Option 2 — Chinese OEM with ARTG registration:**
Some Chinese manufacturers (Mindray, SonoScape, Edan) sell ARTG-registered units through their Australian distribution arms. Source must confirm ARTG listing number before purchase.

**NEVER purchase:**
- Unregistered Chinese import (TGA violation — Class IIa/IIb medical device)
- Equipment without Australian ARTG listing
- Equipment not supported by an Australian sponsor/distributor

**TGA requirement:** All ultrasound machines must be listed on the Australian Register of Therapeutic Goods (ARTG) before supply in Australia. Verify ARTG listing at tga.gov.au before any purchase.

## Key Equipment Specs

- **Probe:** Convex/curved array, 3.5–5 MHz, dedicated obstetric probe
- **Modes:** 3D surface rendering, 4D live (minimum 25 fps), colour Doppler
- **Display:** Minimum 15-inch internal monitor
- **Output:** HDMI to external 60"+ TV for family viewing, USB/SD for image/video capture
- **Software:** HDLive or equivalent 3D rendering, measurement tools, session recording

---

## Scan Room Requirements

- **Size:** 18–20 sqm minimum
- **Lighting:** Dimmable/blackout — low ambient light is critical for image quality
- **TV:** 60-inch minimum wall-mounted — family views scan in real time (emotional = key differentiator)
- **Seating:** Reclining chair for patient + 4–6 chairs for family/friends
- **Decor:** Warm, soft, premium — NOT clinical. No harsh white walls or medical aesthetics.

## Staff Training

**Pathway A — Hire experienced operator:**
- Experienced 3D scan operators exist in Perth (ex-Bloom Baby, Oh Hey Baby, Cedar, 3D Baby Perth)
- These candidates need equipment familiarisation only (~5–10 scans on new machine)
- Start at paid rates from day 1

**Pathway B — Train existing staff member:**
- UltrasoundTrainers.com: Perth WA sessions available, private booking possible
- Cost: A$1,500–4,000 per operator
- Supervised scan requirement: 50–200 scans before independent work
- Cover supervised period with free opening scans

**What training must cover:**
- Machine operation and probe handling
- 3D/4D image optimisation (depth, gain, angle for face detail)
- Gestational age windows (optimal 28–32 weeks)
- Client communication scripts
- Scope of practice: keepsake only — no diagnostic statements ever
- Consent and documentation

**What operator must NEVER say:**
- "Everything looks healthy"
- "No abnormalities"
- Any reference to fetal position, estimated weight, or anatomy being normal/abnormal
- "The baby looks great" (implied diagnostic)

**Correct script:** "This is a keepsake experience — your clinical team manages all medical assessments. Today is about meeting your baby and capturing beautiful memories."

---

## Client Consent Framework

Before every scan, patient signs consent:
1. This is a keepsake service — NOT a diagnostic scan
2. Does not replace clinical ultrasound (anatomy scan, growth scan)
3. Scan operator is NOT a sonographer or clinician
4. Image quality may vary based on fetal position, maternal habitus, gestational age
5. If concerned about fetal wellbeing, contact GP/OB/midwife

## Booking and Add-Ons

Clients plan their visit before arriving:
- Services and add-ons visible at booking (Fresha or booking system)
- Client selects scan package (Peek / Standard / Deluxe) + optional USB / print pack at booking
- Add-ons also selectable on arrival
- Scan scheduled as first service after T+15 — client is emotional and eager

**Best practice:** Book 3D scan as first GTT-window service. Emotional high at that moment drives premium package selection.
