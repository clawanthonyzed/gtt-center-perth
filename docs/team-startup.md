## Part 2: Physical Hire — Who We Need At Venue

### HIRE 1: Receptionist / Manager (Priority: FIRST HIRE)

**Why first:** This person is the backbone. They greet GTT patients, manage bookings, coordinate practitioners, manage the lounge, and report to the Venue Manager. Without this role filled, nothing else works.

**Role title:** Centre Coordinator / Practice Manager
**Award:** Clerks — Private Sector Award MA000002, Level 2-3 (moving to Level 5-6 Hair & Beauty MA000005 if managing beauty staff)
**Employment type:** Permanent part-time OR full-time
**Hours:** 7:00am - 3:00pm (Monday-Friday) to cover full GTT window + afternoon bookings

**Responsibilities:**
- Open venue, prepare lounge, confirm all practitioners are set up
- Check in GTT patients (2-identifier confirmation + consent form)
- Manage booking system (Timely/Phorest) — confirm bookings, fill gaps, handle cancellations
- Coordinate service schedule with practitioners
- Manage food/snack service (lounge)
- Handle retail sales, product hire transactions
- Supervise daily operations when the Venue Manager is not on-site
- Close and lock venue; daily cash reconciliation
- Order consumables and stock
- First-tier complaint handling
- Report weekly to the Venue Manager

**Qualifications sought:**
- Certificate III in Business Administration OR experience in spa/salon management
- Booking software experience (any — train on venue system)
- First aid certificate (required)
- Experience in pregnancy wellness, day spa, medical reception, or allied health preferred
- Calm demeanour (managing pregnant women who have fasted — some may feel unwell)

**Salary:** A$57,000-65,000 + super (permanent, full-time equivalent)
OR A$33.38-38.00/hr casual (loading included)

**Where to recruit:**
- Seek.com.au (search: "spa coordinator Perth", "medical receptionist Perth", "salon manager Perth")
- LinkedIn (Perth beauty/wellness networks)
- Gumtree Perth jobs
- Perth Beauty School alumni networks
- SPA+CLINIC magazine job board (Australian beauty industry)

**Hire timeline:** Begin recruiting in Month 2. Must be employed minimum 4 weeks before venue opens for training.
