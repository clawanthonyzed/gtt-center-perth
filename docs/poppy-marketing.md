## 4. WEBSITE PLATFORM

### Recommendation: Squarespace ($23–33 AUD/month)
- Best balance of design quality and booking integration
- Native integration with Acuity Scheduling (same company)
- Or embed Fresha booking widget directly into Squarespace page
- Good SEO tools built in
- Professional templates for wellness/spa

### Alternative: Webflow (more expensive, more custom)
- $23–39 USD/month
- Better for long-term SEO and custom booking logic
- Requires more technical setup

### Website Must-Haves
- Waitlist form (pre-launch: name, email, suburb, due date, services interested in)
- "How it works" page (GTT procedure + service options in plain language)
- Service menu with pricing
- Embedded booking system (Fresha widget)
- FAQ page (what is a GTT, what to bring, fasting instructions)
- About page (Anthony + Imara story — genuine, personal)
- Partner logos (WDP/PathWest, keepsake scan operator)
- Mobile-first design (90% of traffic will be mobile)

### SEO Targets
| Keyword | Search intent | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| "GTT test Perth" | High — direct service match | P0 |
| "gestational diabetes test Perth" | High — direct | P0 |
| "pregnancy massage Perth" | High | P1 |
| "keepsake ultrasound Perth" | Medium | P1 |
| "3D baby scan Perth" | Medium | P1 |
| "pregnancy wellness Perth" | Medium | P2 |
| "GTT waiting Perth" | Low volume, zero competition | P0 |

**Current competition for "GTT test Perth":** pathology providers (PathWest, WDP) — informational pages only. Zero wellness/experience pages. GTT Center Perth will rank #1 for experience-related searches with basic SEO.

## 8. REFERRAL CARD DESIGN BRIEF

**Size:** A6 (105mm × 148mm) — fits in waiting room holders and pamphlet racks  
**Finish:** Soft-touch laminate, high quality (reflects premium brand)

**Front:**
- Brand name + tagline
- "Turn your GTT wait into your favourite 3 hours of pregnancy"
- Simple illustration of services (massage, scan, nails)
- Booking URL + QR code

**Back:**
- "Your midwife / OB gave you this because they care about your experience"
- 3-step process: Book → Arrive → Glow
- "Perth's first GTT wellness waiting experience"
- Instagram handle, website, phone

**Distribution:** Drop to each midwife + OB in person (or by post for regional/suburban). 50 cards per practice. Restock quarterly.

## 9. CLINIC PARTNER LETTER BRIEF

**Format:** 1 page, A4, professional letterhead  
**Tone:** Colleague-to-colleague, clinical respect, no marketing fluff

**Content:**
1. What GTT Center Perth is (2 sentences)
2. The patient experience problem we solve (fasting, uncomfortable wait)
3. Services offered (list)
4. Referral process (simple: give patient our card / direct them to website)
5. No cost to clinic, no obligation
6. Contact for questions

**No medical claims. No diagnostic claims about 3D scan. No health benefit claims.**
