## How This Differs From `docs/02_issues_and_risks.md`

`02_issues_and_risks.md` is a narrative document from the Phase 1-5 audit pass — it groups findings by High/Medium/Low severity with prose explanations and cross-references to the conflicts log. This document is the same underlying risk universe, restructured into a single flat, ranked table with explicit likelihood/impact/mitigation/owner columns, per the Phase 7 spec. Where a risk appears in both documents, the fuller explanation lives in `02_issues_and_risks.md`; this document is the quick-reference ranked view.
