## Model Confirmation

**Business model:** GTT Center Perth employs all practitioners (casual or part-time).
**Staff per service type:** Launch with 1-2; scale to 3 when capacity data supports.
**Booking window for GTT patients:** T+15 to T+110 (95 usable minutes during 2hr wait).
**Women-focused/safe space:** Partners welcome near cafe/food area only. Not in treatment rooms.
**Children:** Recommended not to attend (customers are there for self-care).

## SERVICE 7: SPRAY TAN (AUTOMATED BOOTH)

**Staff:** Zero — fully automated, self-service
**Equipment:** Enclosed automatic spray tan booth with ventilation/extraction

### Pricing
| Service | Duration | Price |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Express spray tan (booth) | 15 min total | A$50 |
| Spray tan + protective kit (nose filter, hair cap, barrier cream) | 15 min | A$60 |

### Pregnancy Safety Protocol (MANDATORY)
- DHA is surface-only and non-toxic topically; main concern is inhalation of spray mist
- Provide ALL pregnant clients: nose/mouth filter, closed-eye protection, lip balm
- Booth MUST have strong ventilation — minimum 8-10 air changes per hour
- Obtain signed informed consent acknowledging inhalation risk awareness
- **Recommendation:** Position as a non-GTT-day service (offered in afternoon window or on standalone visit days)
- Second and third trimester only for pregnant clients

### GTT Compatibility
- NOT recommended on same day as GTT test (glucose drink + spray tan booth = risk of inhalation discomfort)
- Offer as afternoon/standalone service or next-visit booking
- Available to NON-pregnant clients at any time

### Revenue Potential (Passive — no staff cost)
- A$60/session x 5 sessions/day = A$300/day = A$75,000/year
- Cost per session: under A$5 (DHA solution)
- **Highest ROI service in the venue**
