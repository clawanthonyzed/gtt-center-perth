## 1. BRAND POSITIONING

### Who We Are

GTT Center Perth is Western Australia's first dedicated GTT wellness lounge. We sit at the intersection of clinical care and premium self-care. We are not a pathology lab pretending to be a spa. We are not a day spa pretending to be a clinic. We are something new.

### Brand Pillars

| Pillar | What it means | What it does NOT mean |
|---|---|---|
| **Clinical confidence** | Our phlebotomist is credentialed. Our protocol is NATA-backed. We know what we're doing. | Cold. Institutional. Intimidating. |
| **Premium warmth** | We treat every patient like a guest, not a number. The lounge is beautiful. The snacks are good. | Pretentious. Exclusive. Unaffordable. |
| **Maternal knowing** | We understand what it feels like to be pregnant. We've designed every detail for her. | Condescending. Overly medical. Patronising. |
| **Calm authority** | We don't oversell. We don't panic. We are confident in our offering. | Boring. Flat. Uncommitted. |

### Brand Personality

If GTT Center Perth were a person:
- She is a 35-year-old women's health physiotherapist who also runs a small curated ceramics shop.
- She is warm but not gushing.
- She is professional but not corporate.
- She knows a lot but doesn't show off about it.
- She has excellent taste but doesn't price-gouge.
- She remembers your name.

### Tone of Voice

| Situation | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Website headings | Calm, confident, direct | "The GTT test. Done properly." |
| Instagram captions | Warm, real, a little dry | "You're growing a human. You deserve one hour." |
| Booking confirmation | Reassuring, practical | "Your morning at GTT Center Perth is confirmed. Here's everything you need to know." |
| Clinical information | Clear, plain English, never alarming | "The GTT involves three small blood draws over 2 hours. Most patients find it straightforward." |
| Error or cancellation | Empathetic, solution-focused | "We've had to move your appointment. Here's your new time — and a small thank-you for your flexibility." |

**Voice rules:**
- No exclamation marks in clinical contexts
- No ALL CAPS except in headings
- Active voice always
- Avoid: "amazing", "incredible", "game-changer", "journey", "empowered"
- Use: "calm", "considered", "prepared", "cared for", "known"

## 2. COLOUR PALETTE

### Primary Palette

| Colour | Name | Hex | RGB | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Warm white | **Lounge White** | `#FAF8F4` | 250, 248, 244 | Backgrounds, large surfaces, website BG |
| Sage green | **Sage** | `#8FA98A` | 143, 169, 138 | Primary brand colour, CTAs, logo mark |
| Terracotta | **Terracotta** | `#C47A5A` | 196, 122, 90 | Accent, warmth, highlights, tag lines |
| Warm charcoal | **Deep Slate** | `#2E3330` | 46, 51, 48 | Body text, headings |

### Secondary Palette

| Colour | Name | Hex | RGB | Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Blush | **Morning Blush** | `#F0E0D6` | 240, 224, 214 | Soft background panels, email backgrounds |
| Deep sage | **Forest** | `#5C7A57` | 92, 122, 87 | Hover states, dark-mode sage, logo variants |
| Warm cream | **Parchment** | `#EDE8DF` | 237, 232, 223 | Card backgrounds, intake form BG |
| Off-white text | **Pearl** | `#F5F3EF` | 245, 243, 239 | Text on dark backgrounds |

### Colour Rules

- **Never use pure black (`#000000`) or pure white (`#FFFFFF`)**
- **Primary brand pairing:** Lounge White + Sage + Deep Slate (90% of all design)
- **Terracotta is an accent only** — one element per composition maximum
- **Sage is the only acceptable CTA button colour**
- **No gradients** — solid fills only
- **Minimum contrast ratio:** 4.5:1 for body text (WCAG AA)

### Do Not Use

- Bright pink or hot pink (maternity cliché)
- Clinical blue or hospital green
- Gold or yellow (cheapens the premium positioning)
- More than 3 colours in a single composition

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY

### Typeface — Primary (Display)

**Cormorant Garamond** — Headings, hero text, service names  
Available free: Google Fonts  
Weights used: Light (300), Regular (400), SemiBold (600)  
Style: Elegant, slightly literary, maternal without being fussy

### Typeface — Secondary (Body)

**DM Sans** — Body text, captions, form labels, navigation  
Available free: Google Fonts  
Weights used: Regular (400), Medium (500)  
Style: Legible, contemporary, unpretentious

### Type Scale (website + print)

| Element | Font | Weight | Size (web) | Size (print A4) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hero headline | Cormorant Garamond | Light 300 | 56px | 48pt |
| Section heading (H2) | Cormorant Garamond | Regular 400 | 40px | 34pt |
| Sub-heading (H3) | Cormorant Garamond | SemiBold 600 | 28px | 24pt |
| Body text | DM Sans | Regular 400 | 16px | 11pt |
| Caption / label | DM Sans | Regular 400 | 13px | 9pt |
| Button / CTA | DM Sans | Medium 500 | 15px | 11pt |
| Navigation | DM Sans | Medium 500 | 15px | — |

### Typography Rules

- **Line height:** 1.6 for body text, 1.2 for headings
- **Letter spacing:** +0.02em for DM Sans body, 0 for Cormorant display
- **No bold Cormorant Garamond in body copy** — use DM Sans Medium instead
- **Do not mix more than 2 typefaces** — Cormorant + DM Sans is the complete system
- **Minimum body text size:** 14px web / 10pt print
- **Fallback fonts:** Georgia (serif), system-ui (sans-serif)

## 4. LOGO

### Logo Mark Description

**Primary logo:** "GTT Center Perth" wordmark in Cormorant Garamond Regular + a subtle leaf motif derived from a sage sprig (single stem, 3 leaves, no outline — filled in Sage `#8FA98A`)

**Logo variants:**

| Variant | Use |
|---|---|
| Full wordmark + leaf mark (horizontal) | Website header, letterhead, email signature, signage |
| Stacked wordmark + leaf mark (vertical) | Square social posts, business cards |
| Leaf mark only (icon) | Favicon, app icon, embossed stationery, Instagram profile image |
| Wordmark only (no mark) | Small applications where mark is too small to render clearly (< 80px) |

### Logo Colour Options

| Background | Logo treatment |
|---|---|
| Lounge White / Parchment / Morning Blush | Deep Slate wordmark + Sage leaf mark |
| Sage | Pearl wordmark + white leaf mark |
| Deep Slate | Pearl wordmark + Sage leaf mark |
| Terracotta | Pearl wordmark only (leaf mark loses contrast) |
| Photography / image overlay | Pearl all-white version |

### Clear Space Rule

Minimum clear space around logo = height of the capital "G" in the wordmark on all sides. Nothing may enter this zone.

### Minimum Size

- Print: 30mm wide (full wordmark horizontal)
- Screen: 120px wide (full wordmark horizontal)
- Icon only: no minimum (intended for small applications)

### What NOT to Do

- Do not recolour the leaf mark with terracotta or any non-Sage colour
- Do not use a drop shadow on the logo
- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not place the logo on a busy photograph without the all-white overlay variant
- Do not add a border or box around the logo

> **Note:** Actual logo files (SVG + PNG variants) to be created by designer by Week 5. This section defines the specification for that design brief.

## 5. PHOTOGRAPHY

### Brand Photography Style

**Mood:** Calm. Warm. Intimate. Unhurried.  
**Lighting:** Natural, diffused light. No harsh flash. Golden hour preferred.  
**Setting:** In-venue (lounge chairs, treatment areas, snack station). Never clinical — no needles, collection equipment, or medical paraphernalia visible.  
**Subjects:** Visibly pregnant women (24–34 weeks), diverse ethnicities, Perth metro demographic (28–38 years old), dressed in comfortable but polished clothing (not hospital gowns).

### Shot Types Required (before website launch)

| Shot category | Description | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Hero / full-width | Pregnant woman reclining in lounge chair, soft natural light, relaxed expression | 🔴 Must have |
| Services — massage | Hands on pregnant belly or lower back, oil visible, warm light | 🔴 Must have |
| Services — nails | Close-up of finished manicure, sage or neutral polish, hands resting on visible bump | 🔴 Must have |
| Services — brows | Brow treatment in progress or finished result, soft focus background | 🟡 Need |
| Space — lounge | Wide shot of waiting/service lounge: chairs, plants, snack station visible | 🔴 Must have |
| Space — reception desk | Clean, minimal, welcoming | 🟡 Need |
| Product — snacks | Coconut water, herbal teas, packaged snacks arranged on tray | 🟡 Need |
| Detail — textures | Linen, sage plant, warm ceramic cup, candle — lifestyle details | 🟡 Nice to have |
| Team | Imara and therapist, professional but warm, not posed corporate | 🟡 Need |

### Photography Rules

- **No clinical content** — no needles, vacutainers, collection chairs, sharps containers ever appear in brand photography
- **No filters** — colour correct only; never Instagram filter
- **Consistent warmth** — all images slightly warm-toned, consistent colour grade matching brand palette
- **Aspect ratios to shoot:** 16:9 (website hero), 1:1 (Instagram grid), 4:5 (Instagram portrait)
- **File format:** RAW delivery from photographer, JPEG exports at 80% quality minimum

### Stock Photography Rules

- **Approved sources:** Unsplash, Getty Images (curated search)
- **Search terms:** "prenatal massage", "pregnancy spa", "pregnant woman relaxing", "pregnancy wellness"
- **Avoid stock images that look stock** — no overly posed, bright-lit, fake-smile images
- **Avoid:** Images with visible brands, hospital or clinical setting, non-Perth scenery

### Image Treatment in Design

- **Never crop faces in hero images** — full face visible, not a disembodied bump
- **Darken photo overlays** for text readability: 20–35% Lounge White overlay, not black
- **Never use a black overlay** on brand photography
- **Add subtle warm vignette** (5–10%) to hero images if needed for text contrast

## 6. INSTAGRAM

### Profile

- **Username:** @gttcenterperth
- **Display name:** GTT Center Perth
- **Bio:** "Perth's first GTT wellness lounge. Your 2.5hr test wait, reimagined. 💚 Massage · Nails · Brows · Scan · Dietitian [link]"
- **Profile image:** Leaf logo mark on Lounge White background
- **Link in bio:** Linktree — two options: Join Waitlist + Book Afternoon Session (post-open)

### Grid Aesthetic

**Layout rule:** No strict grid template. Consistent colour palette is enough. Maintain visual warmth.

**Ratio of content types (in rotation):**
- 40% lifestyle/experience (lounge, ambience, details)
- 30% service content (massage, nails, brows — what the services look like)
- 20% educational (GTT preparation, pregnancy wellness tips)
- 10% social proof (re-shares, reviews, team)

### Caption Style

**Length:** Medium — 2–4 sentences + line break + CTA. No essay captions.  
**Emojis:** One emoji maximum per caption. Sage green circle 🟢 or leaf 🌿 preferred. Never an excess of emoji.  
**Hashtags:** 8–12 maximum. Add in a comment, not in the caption body.

**Approved hashtags:**

| Category | Hashtags |
|---|---|
| GTT / GDM | #gestationaldiabetes #gtttest #gttperth #gestationaldiabetestest |
| Pregnancy Perth | #perthpregnancy #pregnantperth #perthmums #perthmaternity |
| Services | #pregnancymassage #pregnancycare #perthspa #maternity |
| Discovery | #perthbusiness #perthlocal #westernaustralia |

### Story Templates

Stories are less formal — real-time, behind the scenes, patient re-shares (with permission). No branded template required for stories. Ensure font used in story text is DM Sans or system default (not Canva decorative fonts).

### Reel Guidelines

- Max 60 seconds for brand reels; 15–30 seconds for service spotlights
- No text-on-screen walls — one statement per cut
- Background music: calm, ambient, no vocals (avoid anything recognisable or gendered)
- First 2 seconds must hook without sound (captions assumed off)

## 9. SIGNAGE AND WAYFINDING

### Exterior Signage

- Logo (full wordmark + leaf mark) on brushed aluminium plate, mounted flush
- Sage on Lounge White or Deep Slate background (building pending, match to façade)
- No illuminated signage (budget constraint — revisit Year 2)

### Interior Wayfinding

| Sign | Location | Treatment |
|---|---|---|
| Welcome / reception | Entrance desk | Framed A3, Cormorant Garamond, Parchment background |
| Collection room | Collection room door | Etched aluminium disc with leaf mark, "Collection Room" in DM Sans |
| "Please knock before entering" | Collection room | Small DL card, slotted holder |
| Bathroom / facilities | Corridor | Brushed aluminium text plate |
| Emergency exit | Exit routes | Compliant green exit signage (WorkSafe WA standard — do not restyle) |

### In-Lounge Details

- Snack station label cards: tent cards, Parchment, DM Sans, product name + allergen note
- Coat hook label: etched brass or brushed aluminium — "Your coat" in Cormorant Garamond

## 11. WHAT GTT CENTER PERTH IS NOT

To keep the brand consistent — especially as partners and third parties produce content about us:

| We are NOT | Therefore |
|---|---|
| A pathology lab | No clinical equipment in brand photography. Never "blood test" in first-touch copy — say "GTT" or "collection" |
| A pregnancy day spa | Don't lead with "spa". Lead with "GTT wellness lounge" |
| A medical clinic | Don't use clinical green, hospital blue, or sterile white |
| Affordable / budget | Don't lead with price. Let the experience justify the cost |
| Exclusive / luxury | "Premium" not "luxury". Don't alienate the A$80 Essential customer |
| A franchise or chain | "Perth's first" reinforces the unique, independent positioning |
