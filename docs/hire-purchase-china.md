## Section 3: China Sourcing — Branding & Wellness Items

### Platform Guide
- **Alibaba.com:** B2B, minimum orders, Trade Assurance recommended. Best for bulk orders.
- **Made-in-China.com:** Good for medical/wellness equipment, alternative to Alibaba
- **1688.com:** Chinese domestic prices (even cheaper) — requires freight forwarder/agent to purchase
- **Key tip:** Always request samples before bulk order. Budget A$50-200 for sample shipping.

### Birth/Wellness Items (Not TGA-regulated — safe to import from China)

Exercise balls, peanut balls, birth combs, acupressure balls, and inflatable birth pools are wellness/fitness items with no TGA registration required. They are subject only to Australian Consumer Law product safety standards.

**Always request from Chinese suppliers:**
- CE or equivalent certification (if electrical)
- REACH compliance certificate (for PVC/rubber items — ensures no phthalates/heavy metals)
- Custom logo branding (silk screening, embossing) — typically free at MOQ 100-200 units
- Safety test report (ISO/ASTM for exercise balls — anti-burst rated at minimum 300kg)

## Section 5: TENS Machine — Regulatory Path

### Option A: Buy ARTG-Registered Australian Brand (Recommended for Launch)
- BabyTENS AU: A$150-200 each x 15 units = A$2,250-3,000 total
- Simple, fast, fully compliant
- Good hire margin (break-even in 2-3 hires)

### Option B: Register Own Brand (If Branding is Important)
- Source TENS machines from China (MOQ 20-50 units, ~A$50-80 each)
- Engage TGA regulatory consultant to register as Australian Sponsor: A$2,000-5,000
- Apply for Class IIa ARTG listing (4-6 weeks): A$1,500-3,000 application fee
- Custom brand (GTT Center Perth TENS) — marketing upside
- **Total cost to register own brand: A$10,000-20,000 upfront + 20-50 units at A$50-80 each = A$11,000-24,000**
- Only worthwhile if selling/hiring at scale (100+ units)

**Recommendation:** Launch with ARTG-registered Australian brand. Review own-brand option at Month 12 if hire volume justifies it.

## Section 6: Freight and Import Logistics

### Recommended Freight Forwarders (Perth)
- DB Schenker Perth (dbschenker.com/en-au)
- Toll Global Forwarding (tollgroup.com)
- Customs Broker: CHX Customs (perth.chx.com.au) — handles import declarations

### Import Duty Rates (Australia 2026)
- Exercise balls, wellness items: 0-5% duty (check FTA with China under ChAFTA)
- Under ChAFTA (China-Australia Free Trade Agreement): many consumer goods are duty-free if supplier provides Certificate of Origin
- GST: 10% on import value (customs) — reclaim as business GST credit on BAS

### Customs Classification (HS Codes)
- Exercise/birth balls: 9506.99 (sports equipment)
- Birth pools (inflatable): 9506.29 (swimming equipment)
- Birth combs/acupressure balls: 9019.10 (massage apparatus)
- Pedicure chairs (electrical): 9402.10 (medical/surgical furniture)
- Spray tan booth: 8516.79 (electro-thermic appliances)
