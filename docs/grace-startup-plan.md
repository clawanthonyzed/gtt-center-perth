- [ ] **GST registration:** Confirm GST registered (mandatory — turnover will exceed $75K). Done via ATO Business Portal.
- [ ] **Business name:** Register "GTT Center Perth" (or chosen brand name) with ASIC under trust ABN. Cost ~$39/yr.
- [ ] **Contact WDP — PRIORITY 1:** WDP explicitly invites Licensed Collection Centre partnerships. Call 08 9317 0999, ask for business development / clinician partnerships. Reference their page: wdp.com.au/clinicians/getting-started-with-us/. This is the single most important call before any lease is signed.

- [ ] **Engage commercial solicitor (Perth):** For Room Licence Agreement template and commercial lease review. Budget ~$1,500–3,000. Find WA solicitor experienced in wellness/medical tenancy.
- [ ] **Engage WA accountant:** Brief on YETI Trust trading GTT Center Perth. Confirm structure, GST, employer obligations. Flag 2028 minimum trust tax change.

## PERTH REAL ESTATE — Target Suburbs and Strategy

### Priority Suburbs (Near Pathology + Hospital Corridor)

| Suburb | Why | Target PathWest/WDP Proximity | LGA |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Subiaco** | Near SJOG Subiaco, multiple OBs/GPs, high SES demographic, good parking | WDP Subiaco, multiple PathWest | City of Subiaco |
| **Nedlands** | Near KEMH, Sir Charles Gairdner, high-income families | PathWest QEII/Sir Charles | City of Nedlands |
| **Osborne Park** | PathWest Osborne Park collection centre already there, easy freeway access, lower rent | PathWest Osborne Park | City of Stirling |
| **Mt Lawley / Highgate** | Growing wellness precinct, younger families, lower rent than Subiaco | Multiple PathWest/WDP | City of Stirling |
| **Victoria Park** | Affordable, growing health precinct, parking, accessible from south | PathWest South Perth nearby | Town of Victoria Park |

### What to Ask the Agent
- Zoning: confirm "Medical Consulting Rooms" or "Health Studio" or "Beauty Salon" permitted use
- Outgoings: get gross rent figure (some Perth commercial leases are net + outgoings)
- Fit-out allowance: negotiate landlord contribution for long-term lease
- Term: 3+3 or 5+5 preferred (break clause at year 2 negotiated)
- Access hours: confirm venue can operate from 7am

### Estimated Rent Ranges (Perth Metro)
- Subiaco/Nedlands: A$450–600/sqm/year (A$4,500–7,000/month for 120 sqm + outgoings)
- Osborne Park: A$250–350/sqm/year (A$2,500–3,500/month for 120 sqm)
- Mt Lawley/Victoria Park: A$280–400/sqm/year (A$2,800–4,000/month for 120 sqm)

*Note: outgoings (rates, insurance, maintenance) typically add 15–25% on top of face rent in Perth retail/commercial.*

### PathWest Perth Metro Collection Centres
- Perth CBD (Wellington St area)
- Osborne Park
- South Perth
- Armadale
- Multiple hospital-based (KEMH, Fiona Stanley, Sir Charles Gairdner, Joondalup)

**Osborne Park or Subiaco are the two highest-value location options.** Osborne Park for cost; Subiaco for demographic and referral access.
