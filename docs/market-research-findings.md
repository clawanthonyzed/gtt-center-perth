## 2. Pathology Fee — OGTT

**Source:** PathWest WA, MBS schedule

### MBS Items (OGTT)
- Item 66551: Glucose tolerance test (fasting + 2hr)
- Item 66554: Glucose tolerance test with additional specimens
- Item 66830: Blood glucose
- Item 71077: Glycated haemoglobin

Frequency limit: 6 times per 12-month period.

### PathWest WA
- Contact: (08) 6383 4234
- PathWest bulk-bills all Medicare-rebateable items for Pensioner/Health Care Card holders
- Specific out-of-pocket cost for non-bulk-billed patients: **not published** — contact PathWest directly

### Action Required Before Pricing Is Finalised
Contact PathWest AND Western Diagnostics Pathology (WDP) to confirm:

2. Collection centre fee structure (collection component vs lab analysis)
3. Whether WDP satellite collection centres can charge an additional venue fee on top

**Estimate pending confirmation:** A$0–80 out-of-pocket depending on bulk-bill status.

## 3. Spray Tan — WA Regulatory Requirements

**Source:** WA Department of Health (health.wa.gov.au/Articles/A_E/Advice-for-the-beauty-therapy-industry)

### WA Requirements
- Pre-service: client skin must be washed with soap/warm water or cleaned with approved antiseptic before service
- **On-site shower mandate:** Not explicitly required under WA health regulations
- Post-spray tan shower: client's responsibility, not mandated on-premises
- ACH (air changes per hour) standard for spray tan booths: no specific WA published standard found
- General salon ventilation safety standards apply

### Action Required
- Contact local council (north or south Perth, whichever location is selected) — requirements vary by council area
- WA Department of Health can provide industry-specific advice: health.wa.gov.au

### GTT Center Perth Implementation
- Dedicated booth with extraction/ventilation (8–10 ACH recommended as best practice)
- Changing area for client
- All pregnant clients: nose/mouth filter + eye protection + lip balm + hair cap — included in service price
- Signed informed consent for pregnant clients (inhalation risk disclosure)

## 4. Belly Casting — Fast-Dry vs Traditional Plaster

**Source:** Natural Beginnings Perth, buddhabellybirth.com, hugomat.com

### Time Comparison
| Method | Setting Time | Full Drying After Removal | Total Session |
|--------|-------------|--------------------------|--------------|
| Fast-dry plaster | 5–15 min | 24–48 hrs | ~1 hr 45 min |
| Traditional plaster | 20–30 min | 24–72 hrs | ~1 hr 45 min |

**Time saving:** Fast-dry saves 5–15 minutes in-session only. Overall session duration nearly identical.
Fast-dry materials cost slightly more per session.

**Recommendation:** Use fast-dry for client comfort (shorter wait during hardening). Price difference in materials is negligible (A$2–5 more per session).

## 6. Eyelash Extensions — Perth Pricing & Duration

**Sources:** perthlashextensions.com.au, eloquenteyes.com.au, waacademy.com, galash.com

### Pricing
| Service | Duration | Perth Price |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Classic full set | 90 min | A$120–150 |
| Hybrid full set | 2–2.5 hr | A$180–220 |
| Volume full set | 2–2.5 hr | A$250–400 |
| Classic infill (50 min) | 50 min | A$99 |
| Hybrid/volume infill | 75 min | A$125 |

### WA Qualification Requirements
- No formal government licence required in WA
- Training recommended: 1–2 days for classic; 2–3 days for hybrid/volume
- Insurance mandatory: Public liability (A$5–10M) + professional indemnity
- Minimum age: 15–16 years

### GTT Center Perth Positioning
- Classic full set (90 min): marginally fits a GTT window (tight — 75 min service window available in Service 2 slot)
- Hybrid/volume: afternoon only (2–2.5 hr)
- Infills: afternoon only

**Our pricing set at market:** Classic A$140, Hybrid A$200, Volume A$280 (slight premium for premium venue).
