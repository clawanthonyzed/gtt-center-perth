## 1. PATHOLOGY PARTNERS

### Western Diagnostic Pathology (WDP) — PRIORITY 1

WDP has a **dedicated Licensed Collection Centre program** — they actively invite medical practices to establish collection points. This is the strongest lead.

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Partnership page** | [wdp.com.au/clinicians/getting-started-with-us/](https://www.wdp.com.au/clinicians/getting-started-with-us/) |
| **Phone** | 08 9317 0999 |
| **Contact page** | [wdp.com.au/contact](https://www.wdp.com.au/contact) |
| **Clinician key contacts** | [wdp.com.au/clinicians/key-contacts/](https://www.wdp.com.au/clinicians/key-contacts/) |
| **Network size** | 200+ locations in WA/NT, Healius group nationally |
| **Core lab** | Myaree (processing) |

**First call script:**
> "We are opening a maternal wellness venue in Perth that hosts GTT appointments. We are looking for a pathology partner to conduct blood collections on-site during the GTT window. We understand WDP establishes Licensed Collection Centres — we'd like to discuss setting one up at our venue."

**What WDP will likely ask:**
- Collection room specification (see research.md §3)
- Expected patient volume per week (start with 20–25/week; grow to 40+)
- Operating hours (7:30am–12:30pm Mon–Fri)
- Insurance certificate for venue
- Whether we will refer all pathology exclusively to them (likely yes for partnership)

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| **Business development contact** | businessdevelopment@clinipath.net |
| **Phone** | (08) 9371 4200 |
| **Sources** | clinipathpathology.com.au/about-us/partner-with-us/, /our-locations/, /contact-us/ |

No published satellite-collection-centre partnership program found (unlike WDP's documented Licensed Collection Centre program) — approach via general business-development inquiry only if WDP and PathWest both decline or terms are unfavourable.

## 3. MASSAGE THERAPIST RECRUITMENT (Perth)

**Target:** Pregnancy massage specialist, own ABN, seeking room rental.

**Where to post / who to contact:**
- **AMT (Australian Massage & Myotherapy Association):** amtaa.com.au — post in their member directory/board
- **ATMS (Australian Traditional Medicine Society):** atms.com.au
- **Facebook Groups to post in:**
  - "Massage Therapists Perth WA" (search Facebook)
  - "Natural Health Practitioners Perth"
  - "Allied Health WA Network"
- **Seek / Indeed:** Post chair rental opportunity (not a job — clearly state independent contractor/chair rental)
- **TAFE WA:** Contact TAFE South Metropolitan (massage therapy program) for recent graduates seeking room rental

**Room rental offer:**
- Rate: A$200–250/week
- Availability: Mon–Fri 7:30am–12:30pm minimum (must be available during GTT window)
- Bring: own equipment preferred, OR use venue table ($20/week consumables fee)
- Requirements: own ABN, public liability insurance ($10M minimum), pregnancy massage qualification
