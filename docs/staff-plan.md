### Phlebotomist (x2 — Chair A and Chair B)
**Status:** Permanent part-time, 2 positions  
**Award:** Health Professionals and Support Services Award MA000027 (HPSS)  
**Classification:** Health Professional Level 2 (Cert III HLT37215 — Pathology Collection)  
**Base rate:** A$27.00/hr (indicative HPSS HP Level 2, 2026 — confirm against current award)  
**Hours:** Mon–Fri 7:30am–12:30pm (25hrs/week)  
**Monthly cost:**
- Base wages: 25hrs × 4.33wks × A$27.00 = A$2,927
- Superannuation (11.5%): A$336
- Leave provisions (10% — annual + sick): A$326
- **Total: ~A$3,589/month**

**Requirements at hire:**
- Cert III HLT37215 Pathology Collection minimum; Cert IV preferred
- Experience with venepuncture, capillary collection, paediatric/maternity settings
- WDP/PathWest credential check (must be approved collector under their NATA accreditation)
- Working with Vulnerable People check (pregnant women)
- Current CPR (HLTAID011)

**Responsibilities:**
- Perform blood collections T=0, T=60, T=120 for all GTT patients
- Prepare and label specimens to WDP/PathWest chain of custody standards
- Operate and maintain collection room to ACSQHC standards
- Administer 75g glucose drink with instructions
- Document collection times accurately
- Courier/dispatch specimens to pathology lab on schedule
- Fasting and adverse event monitoring (patient feels unwell during fast)
- First point of contact for clinical questions during GTT window

**Day 1 model:** 2 phlebotomists employed from launch, each running one collection chair (Chair A handles odd-numbered clients, Chair B even-numbered) — see operations-manual.md for the verified per-client and per-staff timetable. Not a later-stage expansion.

## 6. BREAK-EVEN WITH EMPLOYED MODEL

> **SUPERSEDED** — see financial-break-even-staff.md v2.0 for the current 12-role break-even (~A$775,589/yr, ~29-30 services/day). Retained below for historical reference only.

**Fixed monthly costs (all staff + operations):** ~A$26,500  
**Less sublet income:** −A$2,165  
**Net to cover from package revenue:** A$24,335  
**Package revenue per visit (blended avg):** A$225  
**Break-even visits:** A$24,335 ÷ A$225 = **108 visits/month = 25/week = 5/day**

*5 visits/day is the break-even. Capacity is 8 visits/day. Break-even is 63% utilisation.*
