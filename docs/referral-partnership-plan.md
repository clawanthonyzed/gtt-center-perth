# GTT Center Perth — Referral Partnership Plan (Midwife / OB / GP)

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** 2026-07-19 (Phase 7 new deliverable)
**Purpose:** GP/obstetrician/midwife referral relationship strategy — distinct from `reed-partnerships.md`, which covers pathology-lab (WDP/PathWest/Clinipath) partnership outreach. This document is specifically about the referral network that sends patients to GTT Center Perth, not the pathology partner that processes their blood tests.

---

## Why This Matters

GTT Center Perth's primary customer discovery channel is the referring practice, not paid advertising — per `business-plan.md`'s target customer profile ("referral pathway: midwife, OB/GYN referral, online search, word of mouth") and `afternoon-marketing-plan.md`'s framing ("the main marketing plan is built around midwife/OB referrals → GTT patients → morning shift"). A GTT referral already exists as a mandatory medical event for every pregnant woman in the 24-28 week window — the opportunity is making the referring practice aware that a wellness-integrated option exists for where their patient goes to have it done, not creating demand from nothing.

## Referral Partnership Approach (General Strategy)

Since a full formal outreach program has not yet been executed (this is a planning document, not a report of completed outreach), the approach below is derived from the tactics already documented in `poppy-marketing.md` and `afternoon-marketing-plan.md`, consolidated here:

### Outreach Sequence (per `afternoon-marketing-plan.md` §Partnership Approach)
1. Personal visit or call — not just an email, given the relationship-dependent nature of referral generation
2. In-person introduction (offered as a short, low-commitment "come see what we do" visit)
3. Leave behind: a premium-printed referral card, service menu card, business cards
4. Offer: complimentary wellness visits so the practice's own staff can experience the service firsthand and become genuine advocates, not just distribute cards on faith
5. Follow-up: a "how can we make this work for your clients?" check-in call after a short interval

### Formal Partner Offer (for top referring practices, per `afternoon-marketing-plan.md`)
- Referral credit tracked via the booking system's "How did you hear about us?" field
- Alternative: partner-specific pricing for their clients
- Quarterly in-person check-ins with the top referring practices (Venue Manager, once hired — see `staff-plan.md`)

### Distribution Mechanics (per `poppy-marketing.md` §8 Referral Card Design Brief)
- Referral cards explicitly framed as "your midwife/OB gave you this because they care about your experience" — positions the referring practice as the trusted source, not GTT Center Perth self-promoting
- Distribution: in-person drop to each midwife/OB practice (or by post for regional/suburban), approximately 50 cards per practice, restocked quarterly

**Not yet actioned.** Per `docs/04_roadmap_next_steps.md` and the standing sequencing rule applied throughout this venture's planning: outreach to referring practices is a pre-launch marketing activity (`business-plan.md` §8 Phase 1), which itself depends on a venue location being confirmed and fit-out beginning — it is not an activity that makes sense to start before there's a physical venue and address to refer patients to.
